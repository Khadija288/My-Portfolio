Frontend Developer with Passion for Creating Interactive Web Experiences

Hello! I’m Khadija, a passionate Frontend Developer from Karachi, Pakistan. I specialize in building responsive and visually appealing websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. I'm currently pursuing a diploma in AI Software Engineering from Aptech Metro Star Gate and constantly sharpening my skills to become a full-stack web developer.

My journey started with curiosity and a strong desire to learn, and now I’m turning that passion into real-world web solutions. I'm self-driven, detail-oriented, and always eager to explore new technologies and creative ideas.

I have already built several projects including restaurant websites, bookstores, and mini web apps like digital clocks, to-do lists, and note-keeping tools. Every day, I aim to grow and contribute to meaningful, user-friendly digital experiences.

When I’m not coding, I love learning new things, helping others, and improving myself both technically and spiritually.
